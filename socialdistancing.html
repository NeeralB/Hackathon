About our app

Secure your space is an app that tracks

if you are social SocialDistancing.

Social Distancing is important because many people

are dying and falling sick due to the coronavirus.

Social Distancing is a must, and we decided to

make an app that alerts you if not social distancing.

Secure your Space is the ultimate way to track if

you are social distancing.

We haven't been able to find any website like this yet,

and are thrilled to make a social distancing app.







What it does

This social distancing uses the location and map API's,

and subtracts the coordinates of each person in order to alert someone.

When someone is not properly social distancing or

is closer than 6 feet or 2 meters to someone, a vibrant large button

will pop up on the app, and the user can

tap on the button to make it dissappear. The user can also disable

the app during parties or other events like that.















This the bot that we have made using the pandas library in python. We have incorporated machine learning into this python bot that goes through multiple studies and automatically updates each day about the number of cases and deaths in each place/region
